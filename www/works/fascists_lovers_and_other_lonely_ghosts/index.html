Fascists, Lovers, and Other Lonely Ghosts
2018

An installation about falling in and out of synchrony. A collection of small entities listen to one another and negotiate a collective pulse. But visitors can disrupt or encourage their relationships by moving them around, creating cascades of rhythms.

Thanks: Julius Wilson, Sue Huang, Edek Sher, Greg Picard, Sophia LaCava-Bohanan, Lucia Monge, Paul Rochford.
